Introduction

Welcome to ZenvyDesk. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use ZenvyDesk, an AI-powered desktop application designed to assist users in content creation and workflow management.

By using ZenvyDesk, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services. The types of data we may collect include:

1.1 Account Information

When you log in to ZenvyDesk using Facebook Login, we collect basic profile information such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if provided)
  • Facebook user ID (for authentication purposes)

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to authenticate your account and provide our services.

1.2 Technical and Usage Data

To maintain system stability and improve user experience, we may collect:

  • Device information (operating system, application version)
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs and diagnostic data
  • Session information

1.3 Content Data

When you use ZenvyDesk's AI-powered features to create content, we may temporarily process:

  • Text inputs and prompts you provide
  • Generated content outputs
  • Project and workflow data you create within the application

This data is used solely to provide the AI assistance features and improve content generation quality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Provide core functionality of the application
  • Enable AI-powered content creation features
  • Support workflow management tools

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Improve AI-generated content quality and relevance
  • Enhance user experience and interface design
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns
  • Optimize application performance

2.3 System Maintenance

  • Maintain system stability and security
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms of Service

3. Facebook Login

ZenvyDesk uses Facebook Login as our authentication method. When you log in through Facebook:

  • We only access the minimum information required for authentication
  • We do not post to your Facebook account without your explicit permission
  • We do not access your Facebook friends list, messages, or other unnecessary data
  • You can revoke ZenvyDesk's access at any time through your Facebook settings

Our use of Facebook Login complies with Facebook's Platform Policies and data use requirements.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it:

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure storage systems with access controls
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

4.2 Data Location

Your data may be stored and processed in secure data centers. We ensure that any third-party service providers we use maintain appropriate security standards.

4.3 Limitations

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to maintain the highest standards of data protection.

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5.1 We May Share Data Only When:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud hosting) who assist in operating our application. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

  • Protection of Rights: We may share information to protect the rights, property, or safety of ZenvyDesk, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5.2 No Marketing Use

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active and you continue to use ZenvyDesk.
  • Deleted Accounts: When you request account deletion, we remove your personal data within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Legal Obligations: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as resolving disputes or enforcing agreements).
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you personally for analytical purposes.
