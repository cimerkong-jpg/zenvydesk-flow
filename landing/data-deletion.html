Data Deletion Instructions

Introduction

At ZenvyDesk, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you would like to request deletion of your data associated with ZenvyDesk, this page provides clear instructions on how to do so.

We are committed to processing data deletion requests promptly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Your Right to Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. When you submit a deletion request, we will remove your account information and associated personal data from our systems, subject to certain legal and operational requirements described below.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your ZenvyDesk data using one of the following methods:

Method 1: Email Request

Send a data deletion request to: support@zenvydesk.site

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your account email address or Facebook account identifier
  • A clear statement that you want your data deleted
  • Any additional information that helps us verify your identity

Subject line: "Data Deletion Request - ZenvyDesk"

This helps us process your request more quickly and ensures it reaches the right team.

Method 2: Facebook App Settings

Since ZenvyDesk uses Facebook Login for authentication, you can also remove ZenvyDesk's access to your Facebook account:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "ZenvyDesk" in the list of connected apps
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke ZenvyDesk's access

Note: Removing the app from Facebook will prevent ZenvyDesk from accessing your Facebook data, but to ensure complete deletion of data stored in ZenvyDesk's systems, we recommend also submitting an email request (Method 1).

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and security, we need to verify your identity before processing a data deletion request. This verification process may include:

  • Confirming your email address matches our records
  • Verifying your Facebook account information
  • Requesting additional information if needed to confirm your identity

We will contact you if we need additional information to verify your request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove the following information from our systems:

Account Information

  • Your name and email address
  • Facebook user ID and authentication data
  • Profile information
  • Account preferences and settings

Content and Usage Data

  • Content you created using ZenvyDesk
  • Project and workflow data
  • Usage history and activity logs
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

In certain circumstances, we may retain some data even after a deletion request:

Legal and Compliance Requirements

  • Legal Obligations: Data required to comply with legal obligations, such as tax records or data needed for ongoing legal proceedings
  • Security Logs: Minimal security and audit logs may be retained temporarily for fraud prevention and system security purposes
  • Anonymized Data: Data that has been fully anonymized and cannot identify you personally may be retained for analytical purposes

Retention Period

Any data retained for legal or security purposes will be kept only for the minimum period required by law or legitimate business needs, typically no longer than:

  • Security logs: 90 days
  • Legal compliance data: As required by applicable law
  • Anonymized data: Indefinitely (as it cannot identify you)

Processing Time

We are committed to processing data deletion requests promptly:

  • Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 2-3 business days
  • Verification: Identity verification typically takes 1-3 business days
  • Deletion: Once verified, data deletion is completed within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You will receive confirmation once the deletion process is complete

Total timeframe: Most deletion requests are completed within 30 days of verification. In some cases, it may take longer if additional verification is required or if we need to comply with legal obligations.

Effect of Data Deletion

After your data is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to access your ZenvyDesk account
  • All content and projects you created will be permanently removed
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use ZenvyDesk again in the future
  • Any data you previously shared with third parties (such as content posted on social media) will not be affected, as it is outside our control

Important Considerations

Backup and Recovery

Before requesting deletion, please note that:

  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • We recommend backing up any content you wish to keep before submitting a deletion request
  • Once deleted, we cannot recover your data

Third-Party Content

If you have used content created with ZenvyDesk on third-party platforms (such as social media), deleting your ZenvyDesk data will not remove that content from those platforms. You will need to manage that content separately according to each platform's policies.

Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about the data deletion process or need assistance with your request, please contact us:

Email: support@zenvydesk.site

Our support team is available to help you through the process and answer any questions you may have about data deletion.

Your Privacy Rights

Data deletion is just one of your privacy rights. For more information about how we handle your personal data, please review our:

  • Privacy Policy - Learn about our data practices
  • Terms of Service - Understand your rights and responsibilities

Compliance

Our data deletion process complies with:

  • Facebook Platform Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations

We are committed to respecting your privacy rights and handling your data responsibly.
